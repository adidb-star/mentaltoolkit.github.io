Screen-Time Detox Plan

  • Set Daily Screen Time Limits: Limit non-essential screen use like social media to maintain a healthy balance.
  • Use “Do Not Disturb” Mode: Turn on during study or before bed to minimize distractions and help focus or relax.
  • Take Regular Digital Detox Breaks: Step away from screens every few hours for at least 30 minutes to rest eyes and mind.
  • Replace Screen Time with Relaxing Activities: Swap screens for offline hobbies like reading, journaling, or walking.
  • Avoid Screens 1 Hour Before Bed: Blue light can disrupt sleep, so stop screens early to improve sleep quality.
  • Turn Off Non-Essential Notifications: Disable unnecessary alerts to reduce distractions.
  • Use Apps to Monitor and Limit Usage: Track screen time and set limits with built-in phone tools or apps.
  • Create Screen-Free Zones: Make areas like dining tables or bedrooms screen-free to encourage mindful use.
  • Practice Mindful Screen Use: Ask if using your device is necessary before picking it up to avoid unconscious scrolling.
  • Involve Family or Friends: Detox together to stay motivated and make it more enjoyable.
