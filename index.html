About This Project

This toolkit was created by Aditya Dev Biswas a student at JSSPS Dubai with a goal to provide support in a simple manner. It aims to provide simple and practical tools to help fellow teens manage stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges. The MindEase Toolkit is designed to support mental well-being by offering easy exercises, helpful tips, and useful resources that anyone can use to feel more balanced and confident every day.It was designed to be an easy-to-access website that is simple and effortless to navigate.
