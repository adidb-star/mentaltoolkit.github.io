Breathing Exercises

  • Box Breathing (Square Breathing): Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds. Repeat 4 times. Helps reduce stress and improve focus.
  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale 4 seconds, hold 7 seconds, exhale 8 seconds. Repeat 3–4 times. Calms anxiety and aids sleep.
  • Deep Belly Breathing (Diaphragmatic Breathing): Breathe deeply into your belly, keeping chest still. Exhale slowly. Activates relaxation and improves oxygen flow.
  • Alternate Nostril Breathing (Nadi Shodhana): Close right nostril, inhale left; close left nostril, exhale right; reverse. Repeat 5 minutes. Balances nervous system and mind.
  • Resonant Breathing (Coherent Breathing): Inhale for 5 seconds, exhale for 5 seconds at a smooth pace. Enhances calmness and mood.
  • Pursed Lip Breathing: Inhale slowly through nose, exhale through pursed lips for twice as long. Helps control shortness of breath and relax.
  • Equal Breathing (Sama Vritti): Inhale for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds. Maintains balance and focus.
  • Stimulating Breath (Bellows Breath / Bhastrika): Rapid, forceful inhales and exhales through nose like bellows. Energizes body and clears mind (use sparingly).
  • Sighing Breath: Take a deep breath and release with a long audible sigh. Releases tension and stress.
  • Progressive Relaxation Breathing: Inhale deeply, tense a muscle group, hold 5 seconds, exhale and relax. Repeat moving upward. Combines breathing with muscle relaxation.
